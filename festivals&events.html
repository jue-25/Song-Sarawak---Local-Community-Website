Song, Kapit's Festivals & Events



Pesta Dabai Song (Song Dabai Festival)

One of the specialties and uniqueness of the Song District is dabai fruit. The Song district is very famous for a type of exotic fruit, which is dabai or its scientific name ‘Canarium Odontophyllum’. Dabai fruits are often in high demand because of the softness of the filling and the tastier taste compared to dabai in other places in Sarawak. The district is well-known for the Sarawak state's best quality ‘dabai’. Pesta Dabai features a Dabai exhibition as well as cooking competitions, games, colouring contests, futsal competition, performances, food fairs, and many more.
